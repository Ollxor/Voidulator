Expanding rings from their own emitters (teal dots). Lifetime in bounces: 1 = reach the wall, 2 = back through center. Range sliders pick a random value between the two markers.
Wave Field
1
2
A real wave-equation simulation inside the room. Emitters are wave sources; click the stage to drop a ripple. Reflecting walls give resonances — try the ellipse with a source on a focus. Inspired by Nils Berglund's simulations.
Generative
A gallery of original fullscreen shaders standing in for beams/rings — no room geometry involved. Works with Glow and Kaleidoscope like every other mode.
Trails
Length: trail duration. Hue shift: 0=same color, 180=complementary. Filtering: Smooth re-blends sub-pixel detail each frame (feeds the interference patterns thin slow beams make); Crisp keeps pixels exact. Cutoff: lower keeps faint layers alive longer.
Glow
Real laser glow — bright beams bleed light like through haze. Threshold: how bright a pixel must be to bloom.
Kaleidoscope
6
N-fold radial mirror of the whole frame — stacks with beams, rings, and the wave field. Mirror off = a plain rotational repeat instead of a reflected fold.
Phosphor Walls
Beams paint glow onto the walls where they strike; it fades over Persistence seconds. Great with bent walls — the hot spots reveal the caustics.
